title:  "Skin"
date:   2019-09-25 20:26:58 -0700
categories: Poem
---

A fruit lay
translucent cells, exposed to the sun.
Growing, undisturbed,
precious interior, on full display.

Animals, creatures with strength,
demand nourishment.
Harvesting it, for their own,
exploiting the plant at full length.
